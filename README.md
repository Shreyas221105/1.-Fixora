# 1.-Fixora
“Fixing homes, simplifying lives.”



Our startup aims to simplify everyday life by providing a single platform to access essential home services such as carpentry, plumbing, cleaning, electrical repairs, and more.

In today’s fast-paced world, finding reliable service professionals can be time-consuming and stressful. Our platform solves this problem by connecting users with verified, skilled, and trusted service providers in just a few clicks.

Users can easily:

Browse a wide range of services
Book professionals instantly or schedule appointments
Compare prices and ratings
Track service status in real time
Make secure payments

On the other hand, service providers get a digital platform to grow their business, manage bookings, and reach more customers.

💡 Vision
To become the go-to platform for every household need, making home services accessible, reliable, and hassle-free.

🎯 Mission
To empower local service providers while delivering convenience, trust, and quality service to customers.
